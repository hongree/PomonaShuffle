The Pomona Shuffle

The Pomona Shuffle is an interactive class-finder for the eager, wise, and slightly area-deficient Sagehen. Click on each card to scroll through the current list of classes. Press space to bring up ten new courses currently offered for Fall 2013!

Pomona Shuffle is currently displaying all 2200+ courses available to all students at the 5Cs. Come back next semester for more updates!

Email PomonaShuffle@gmail.com with any questions/concerns you may have!

Pomona Shuffle was inspired by Geoffrey Litt's Yale ClassRoulette.

Designed by Aloke Desai '16, Hong Suh '16, Mauricio Molina '15, and Ryan Luo '16.
